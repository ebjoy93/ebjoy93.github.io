City Latitude vs. Wind Speed (08/22/18)

Based on the data analysis, it appears that there is a correlation between latitude and wind speed. When viewing the plot, cities at an latitude between 0 and 60, experience about the same wind speed. There is no correlation between latitude and high wind speeds.
